

The pension increase window will open in 2021. "Economic reference" reporters learned that China's urban retirees' pension is about to usher in a "17 consecutive increases."The specific increase plan is in the pipeline and is expected to be released within this month. At the same time, the pension treatment of urban and rural residents is also expected to be increased, and many places have been vigorously promoting it.

The signal of accelerating the reform of the old-age insurance system is also being intensively released. The National overall planning of old-age insurance is on the line, and the individual pension system is also being studied and formulated.

By 2020, China's pension for retirees has achieved "16 consecutive increases." Since 2018, it has maintained an increase of about 5% for three consecutive years. The 2021 government work report proposes to increase the basic pension for retirees. According to the recent State Council's opinions on the division of key work in the implementation of the "government work report,"the pension for retirees will continue to be increased this year. It is clear that the Ministry of Human Resources and social Affairs and the Ministry of Finance will take the lead. Relevant policies will be issued by the end of April and will continue to be promoted throughout the year.

For the pension increase in 2021, the industry expects it to remain within 5% .

"After years of upward adjustment, the absolute value of pension significantly increased, Consumer Price Index is not high, the increase in 5% or slightly slowed down, the absolute amount will significantly increase, the purchasing power of pension steadily increased, not only can ensure that the retirement living standard does not decline, but also can make the pension insurance Fund more sustainable."Chinese Academy of Social Sciences world Social Security Research Center executive researcher Zhang Yinghua told the" economic reference "reporter.

In addition to the steady increase in pensions for retirees, pensions for urban and rural residents will also be increased. Data show that by the end of 2020, the number of people participating in the basic old-age insurance for urban and rural residents in China exceeded 542 million, most of whom were rural residents. In 2020, governments at all levels will provide more than 300 billion Yuan of old-age insurance subsidy funds for urban and rural residents. In addition to the increase in local financial subsidies and the accumulation of individual contributions, the per capita pension level for urban and rural residents has been increased to 170 Yuan per month by the end of 2020.
